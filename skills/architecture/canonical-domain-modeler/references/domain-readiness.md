# Domain Readiness

The inputs are ready for canonical domain modeling when most of the following are true:

- the main business workflow is known
- the key business nouns are visible
- important actors are understood
- at least one major lifecycle is clear
- core rules and constraints are partially concrete
- failure or exception paths are not hidden

Not ready signals:

- the requirements are still mostly UI-oriented
- core nouns are unstable or contradictory
- the workflow is too vague to identify ownership or lifecycle
- rules are implied but never stated
- it is impossible to tell what must remain consistent

If not ready, ask follow-up questions instead of forcing a model.
