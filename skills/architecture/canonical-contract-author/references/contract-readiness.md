# Contract Readiness

The inputs are ready for canonical contract authoring when most of the following are true:

- the main commands and queries are already known
- the main domain concepts and statuses are stable
- important failure modes are visible
- retry-sensitive operations are known
- the use-case model is stable enough to map externally

Not ready signals:

- commands still have unstable names
- business outcomes are unclear
- failure semantics are hidden
- status vocabularies are still changing
- the use-case model is incomplete or inconsistent

If not ready, ask follow-up questions before drafting the contract.
