# Gap Quality Gate

Review the analysis against this checklist.

## Gap Quality

- Are the gaps concrete rather than generic?
- Are contradictions called out explicitly?
- Are blocking and deferrable gaps separated?

## Prioritization Quality

- Are the highest-impact issues first?
- Is the ranking justified by downstream effect?

## Question Quality

- Are the follow-up questions specific and high leverage?
- Is the number of questions restrained?
- Do the questions clearly map back to the identified gaps?

## Progress Quality

- Does the analysis preserve momentum instead of restarting broad discovery?
- Are bounded assumptions proposed where appropriate?

## Reject Conditions

Revise the analysis if it reads like:

- a generic brainstorm list
- a copy of the source material with comments
- a huge undifferentiated questionnaire
- a critique with no concrete next step
