# Gap Categories

Use these categories when analyzing incomplete requirements.

## Missing Information

A key concept or rule is simply absent.

## Ambiguous Terminology

The meaning of a term is unclear, overloaded, or unstable.

## Conflicting Statements

Two sources describe different behaviors, rules, or boundaries.

## Underspecified Workflow

A workflow exists, but:

- entry conditions are unclear
- outcomes are unclear
- actors are unclear
- exception paths are missing

## Missing Policy or Invariant

A concept exists, but the rules that constrain it are not explicit enough.

## Missing Failure Path

The happy path exists, but blocked, rejected, retried, or partially failed behavior is absent.

## Missing Scope Boundary

Goals exist, but non-goals or external boundaries are missing.

## Missing Read Need

The system must expose operational or reporting views, but the read-side needs are vague or absent.
