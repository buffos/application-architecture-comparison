# Review Format

Use this structure for responses.

## When Findings Exist

List findings first.

Each finding should include:

- severity
- affected artifact(s)
- issue
- impact
- recommendation or clarification needed

After findings, optionally include:

- open questions
- explicit assumptions
- short readiness summary

## When No Findings Exist

State explicitly that no material findings were identified.

Then include:

- residual risks
- testing gaps
- short readiness summary

## Severity Guidance

- `High`: likely to cause divergent implementations or major rework
- `Medium`: likely to confuse design, testing, or comparison work
- `Low`: minor ambiguity or cleanup issue with limited behavioral impact
