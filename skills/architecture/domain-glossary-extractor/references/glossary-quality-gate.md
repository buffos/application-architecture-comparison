# Glossary Quality Gate

Review the glossary against this checklist.

## Coverage Quality

- Are the main actors present?
- Are the main business objects present?
- Are important statuses and policy terms present?

## Clarity Quality

- Does each canonical term have a precise meaning?
- Are aliases and discouraged terms handled explicitly?
- Are critical distinctions visible?

## Stability Quality

- Would downstream documents be able to use this vocabulary consistently?
- Are unresolved terminology conflicts surfaced rather than hidden?

## Neutrality Quality

- Does the glossary avoid UI-first or implementation-first naming?
- Does it stay anchored in business semantics?

## Reject Conditions

Revise the glossary if it reads like:

- a feature list
- a class list
- a UI menu list
- or an unordered bag of nouns without definitions
